GOOD HURT FUEGO

How do you take a plain bottle of hot sauce and transform it into an "O Magazine" success story?

Go To Website

MY ROLE

Creative Direction.

THE CHALLENGE

Design Priorities:
1. Create a name for this product.
2. Create a playful presentation that would catch the eye of Oprah's team.
‍
Brainstorming the name:
Hot sauce is a good kind of pain. It's tasty, but boy it hurts.

Getting into "O" Magazine: During this time, it was well known that Oprah and Beyoncé were friends. And in the brainstorming session I looked into songs from Beyoncé's album "Lemonade". The lyrics “I got hot sauce in my bag/swag” stood out for obvious reasons and ideas quickly evolved from the bomb to a stack of dynamite.
